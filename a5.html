serenity


  Serenity.
  Serenity, the State of
  being calm,
  peaceful
  and Untroubled.
  through all the chaos,
  Chaos of the past, Today,
  neck Deep in mud
  Of human Production.

  Untouched. untouched
  by the greed.
  Purity, soon Gone,
  innocent Wonder
  Evaporated Mist.
  Chaos, the lone wolf
  accompanying Humanity
  into its Death
  Suffocating.
